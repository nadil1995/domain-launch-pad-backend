Opening: Ruy Lopez (Spanish Opening)

1. e4 - White opens with the king's pawn, the most popular first move
2. e5 - Black mirrors White's move, establishing central control
3. Nf3 - White develops the knight, attacking Black's e5 pawn
4. Nc6 - Black defends the e5 pawn and develops a piece
5. Bb5 - The Ruy Lopez! White pins the knight to the king
6. a6 - Black challenges the bishop with the Morphy Defense
7. Ba4 - The bishop retreats but maintains pressure
8. Nf6 - Black develops the kingside knight
9. O-O - White castles kingside for king safety
10. Be7 - Black prepares to castle as well
